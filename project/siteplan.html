GameForge X: Site Plan

Site Plan

GameForge X – The ultimate hub for gamers and tech-heads. Your one-stop spot for gaming news, patch updates, gear reviews, and the latest tech gadgets.

Site Purpose

• Deliver latest gaming news, updates, and patches
• Highlight must-have gaming gear (mice, keyboards, headsets, controllers)
• Showcase other tech gadgets for office, streaming, or content creation (monitors, webcams, microphones, smart lights)
• Feature community reviews and tips
• Provide comparison guides for both gaming and tech gear

Scenarios

  • “Which gaming mouse or keyboard is best for FPS games?”
  • “Where can I see real user reviews for gaming laptops?”
  • “What’s the latest patch/update for Fortnite, Call of Duty, or FIFA?”
  • “Which gaming headset gives the best sound without breaking the bank?”
  • “Which office or streaming gadget can level up my setup?”

Color Scheme

Primary: #1f1f1f (background)
Accent 1: #00ff99 (highlights, buttons)
Accent 2: #ff4d4d (alerts, trending)
Accent 3: #00dfff (tech/gadgets)

Typography

Roboto – Body text

Bebas Neue – Headings, section titles

Orbitron – Optional for product highlight titles
